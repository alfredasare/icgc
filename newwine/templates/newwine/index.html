DON'T WANT TO BE LEFT OUT ?

JOIN A COVENANT FAMILY

In New Wine, we have cell groups known as Covenant Families. Every member belongs to a Covenant Family by virtue of the hall or hostel where he or she resides. In our families, we pray, break bread, fellowship and learn so many things from one another. Trust me, you don't want to miss out on this!

JOIN A DEPARTMENT

Every member is expected to serve in a department. It is a privilege to be called by God to serve. Your service in a department helps greatly in running the affairs of God's house. Don't be a benchwarmer, participate in departmental activities and remember, your act of service will never be in vain.

KNOW YOUR EXECUTIVES

God has elected shepherds to watch over our souls as New Wine. These are students, who have many academic duties to perform just like you, yet have availed themselves to serve you and New Wine. Getting to know them doesn't hurt. Just follow the link in the navigation to find out more about them
